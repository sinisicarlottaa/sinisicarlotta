Project Overview

Website created for a professional in the psychological field, with the goal of presenting services, articles, and contact information in a clear and accessible way. This is a personal project developed for an acquaintance.

Identity

The website’s identity was designed to convey competence, active listening, and care. The tone of voice is calm, reassuring, and direct, offering a safe space where users can feel understood.

Website Sections

  • Home – presentation and therapeutic approach
  • Services – areas of intervention
  • Articles – educational content
  • Footer – contact information

Technologies

Developed using Angular (latest versions), SCSS for styling, and Bootstrap to ensure responsive layouts and consistency across devices.
